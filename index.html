Latacunga

Gateway to the Avenue of Volcanoes

Nestled in the heart of Ecuador's Avenue of Volcanoes, Latacunga is a charming colonial city with rich cultural heritage, vibrant markets, and extraordinary natural beauty. Serving as the perfect base for exploring Cotopaxi National Park, Latacunga invites you to experience its unique blend of indigenous traditions, Spanish colonial architecture, and warm Ecuadorian hospitality.

During your visit, you'll discover the city's remarkable historical center, taste authentic local cuisine including the famous chugchucaras, and embark on adventures into the surrounding highlands and volcanic landscapes.

Guaranda

The City of Seven Hills

Known as "The City of Seven Hills," Guaranda captivates visitors with its colorful colonial architecture, vibrant carnival celebrations, and breathtaking views of the majestic Chimborazo volcano. This charming provincial capital in Ecuador's central highlands offers a perfect blend of cultural richness and natural beauty.

During your stay, you'll explore cobblestone streets lined with pastel-colored buildings, visit the famous Seven Colored Corner, and experience the warm hospitality of the local Bolívar province people. Guaranda is also known for its exceptional craftsmanship and delicious local specialties like the famous "Pájaro Azul" aniseed liquor.

Chillanes

Ecuador's Hidden Paradise

Nestled in the lush subtropical region between the Andes and the coastal plains, Chillanes is one of Ecuador's best-kept secrets. This picturesque town offers a perfect blend of stunning natural landscapes, traditional rural life, and authentic cultural experiences away from the usual tourist trail.

During your stay, you'll discover breathtaking waterfalls, hike through pristine cloud forests teeming with unique flora and fauna, and experience the genuine hospitality of the local community. Chillanes is also known for its agricultural bounty, producing some of Ecuador's finest coffee, sugar cane, and tropical fruits.
